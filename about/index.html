About

I am Rajkumar, an ambitious software engineer, ever in awe of the wonders that 0s & 1s could create. I started my development with mainly Android apps made out of Java, then got to explore web technologies such as Laravel, NodeJS, Angular etc.

The software I mostly use are :

  • Jetbrains suite of IDEs for development.
  • Sublime Text for quick text editing.
  • Sublime Merge for complex git stuff.
  • Duckduckgo mostly, Startpage rarely.

Linux ♥ Open Source

Ever since I felt bad/disgusted about using non-activated Windows in my first year of college, I switched to Linux ♥ (Ubuntu initially) and never looked back. During my four years of college, I have hopped between multiple Linux distributions and out of everything, my favourite has been elementaryOS because it’s simple, clean and less intrusive thus letting me be more productive.

Apple 👨🏻‍💻

I am also a huge fan of Apple products, and their obsession in UI detailing & providing a seamless user experience across their products. I currently possess an iPhone XR, and a MacBook Air (i5 10th gen) for my daily personal usage.

Security x Privacy 👻
